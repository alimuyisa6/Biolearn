Submission Guidelines

Help us maintain quality resources for biology and pharmacy learners.

How to Submit

1

Sign In

Create an account or sign in to access the submission form.

2

Prepare Content

Ensure your notes are accurate, well-formatted, and original.

3

Fill the Form

Provide a clear title, description, and file details.

4

Submit for Review

Our team reviews all submissions within 48 hours.

What We Accept

Original biology or pharmacy notes for O-Level or A-Level
Past papers with answer keys
Well-organized PDF documents with clear diagrams
Study guides and revision materials

What We Do Not Accept

Copyrighted material from other websites without permission
Incomplete or low-quality scans
Content unrelated to biology or pharmacy
Duplicate submissions already in our library

Tips for Quality Submissions

  • Use clear, descriptive titles (e.g., "O-Level Cell Biology Notes — Transport Mechanisms")
  • Include author name and institutional affiliation if applicable
  • Provide accurate file size and format information
  • Add relevant tags to help students find your resource
  • Review your content for accuracy before submitting
